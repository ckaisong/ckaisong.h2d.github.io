About Hash2day
	
1) Can I join a mining pool through Hash2day?
	
Yes, all Hash2day customers can select a mining pool of their own choice prior to installation of your mining hardware. 
Our Mining Concierge team will email you during the week leading up to the install date. After you have started mining, 
you can also change what mining pool you join by submitting a ticket to our Help Center.

3) Does Hash2day require identity verification?
	
Hash2day requires your name and email address for purchases using debt, credit or wire transfers with Stripe.

Getting started

1) Payment Methods

Hash2day processes fiat payments through Stripe. Customers can pay via debit, credit or bank wire. 
Please note a 4% fee is included on credit card purchases. 
Hash2day also offers customers the ability to pay in Bitcoin (BTC), Tether (USDT) or USD Coin (USDC) through coinpayments.com.


2) Choosing a Mining Pool
Each mining pool is unique, and miners should perform due diligence before joining a mining pool. 
Hash2day can assist in both making a mining pool account and linking your machine to the pool.


3) Learn more about mining pools
Hash2day encourages miners to choose any mining pool of their liking in order to promote the continued decentralization of the Bitcoin network. 
However, Hash2day customers can get pool fee discounts when joining certain pools. Please contact the Hash2day team for more information on current discounts.


4) Choosing an ASIC
Machines with high hashrate and lower power consumption are best. Bitmain (Antminer), MicroBT (WhatsMiner), 
and Canaan (AvalonMiner) are the most well-known bitcoin ASIC manufacturers.
Always perform due diligence and do your own research before purchasing.

Create a Hash2day Account
You can create a Hash2day account directly from our website. Check on orders and active machines, 
purchase more equipment, review available hosting facilities or contact support from your account.


Managing my account

1) Changing Mining Pool Account

Hash2day customers can change their mining pool account at any time by submitting a ticket to our Help Center.


2) Update Account Settings

You are able to change your email and password by heading to the Settings menu on the sidebar of the Hash2day Dashboard.


3) Navigating Dashboard

Once you create an account, you have access to the Hash2day Dashboard. The dashboard shows your purchased machines, hosting facility information, 
hosting rate and mining pool. You are also able to submit a support ticket on the top right corner of the screen.
